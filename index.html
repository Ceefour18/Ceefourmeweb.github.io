Meet Ceefour

As a Clinical Data Scientist and Bioinformatics Specialist, I leverage my background in biological sciences to transform complex biological data into actionable clinical insights. My journey has evolved from traditional statistics (SPSS, Epi Info, STATA) to advanced data science using Python, R, and SQL, now focused on Generative AI, AI agent design, and comprehensive bioinformatics pipelines.

I specialize in building intelligent healthcare solutions that automate clinical workflows, enhance decision-making, and drive research innovation through state-of-the-art AI architectures and genomic analysis.

Core Expertise

  • Bioinformatics: QC, Alignment, BAM, Variant calling, Downstream analysis, NGS, Genomics Workflows, Sequencing Pipelines, Sequence Alignment & Phylogenetic Analysis.
  • Artificial Intelligence: Machine Learning, Agentic AI Architectures, Generative AI, LLM Fine-tuning (Med-PaLM, ClinicalBERT).
  • Biostatistics: Linear, log-binomial, negative binomial, poisson regression, multinomial regression, cox regression and other Generalized Linear Models.
  • Data Science: Predictive Modeling, Clinical NLP, Data Visualization.
  • Technology Stack: Python, R, SQL, Linux, Cloud Platforms, Bash Scripting & Automation.
  • Domain Knowledge: Bioinformatics Database Use, HIPAA/GDPR Compliance, Ethical AI in Healthcare.
